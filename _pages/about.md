I am Rhea Sukthanker and I am a PhD Student at the [Machine Learning Lab, University of Freiburg](https://ml.informatik.uni-freiburg.de/), supervised by [Prof. Dr. Frank Hutter](https://ml.informatik.uni-freiburg.de/profile/hutter/). Prior to starting my PhD studies I completed my masters at ETH Zurich where I worked at the [Computer Vision Lab](https://vision.ee.ethz.ch/) in the area of Neural Architecture Search and Normalizing Flows. I was supervised at CVL by [Dr. Zhiwu Huang](https://zhiwu-huang.github.io/) and [Dr. Suryansh Kumar](https://suryanshkumar.github.io/). My research interests broadly lie in the area of Efficient and Multi-Objective Neural Architecture Search. I am also interested in Parameter-Efficient Fine-Tuning and specificially fine-tuning to align with human values (architecture efficiency, fairness, robustness). When I am not coding or reading papers I like to spend my time going for long walks in nature, writing poetry or experimenting with new recipes.
